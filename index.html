About Your Coach

Meet Shamar

I started coaching because I got tired of watching people fail on programs that were never built for them. Every plan I write starts with a conversation, not a template — your schedule, your injuries, your goals, your history with training. That's what actually gets results that stick.

Whether you're stepping into a gym for the first time or chasing a specific number on the bar, the approach is the same: clear programming, honest feedback and a plan you can actually follow.

  • Certified Personal Trainer & Strength Coach
  • Specializes in fat loss, hypertrophy & strength cycles
  • Coaching style in-person + fully remote via app
